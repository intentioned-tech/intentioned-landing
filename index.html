Important Clarifications

*Important Clarifications:

  • "No data leaks" refers to your conversation data processed by the Intentioned application. Your local machine's security is your responsibility. If you configure external LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.), your prompts and data are sent to those services‚Äîplease review their respective privacy policies. This claim also does not apply to payment processing or other interactions with our site. See our privacy policy and terms of use for more information.
  • "No cloud" and "no latency" refer to the core application interface running locally. External services like payment processing, mailing list, and license validation operate via cloud and are GDPR-compliant. Data collected for these services can be deleted upon request. The built in content moderation, if enabled, may send data to the app operator but not Intentioned. This is toggleable but on by default. Always check the setting of the content moderation before using the app.
  • External LLMs: Intentioned supports both local and cloud-based LLMs. When using cloud LLMs, your prompts may contain conversation context. We recommend using local models for maximum privacy.
  • Payment & Services: Payment processing, mailing list subscriptions, and license activation data are stored securely in our cloud infrastructure. This data is GDPR-compliant and can be deleted upon request by contacting us.
